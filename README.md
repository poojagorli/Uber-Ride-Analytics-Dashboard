# 🚖 Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

The Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built using Power BI to analyze ride booking performance, revenue trends, customer behavior, vehicle utilization, and regional demand patterns.

This dashboard transforms raw ride-booking data into meaningful insights, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve operational efficiency and business growth.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem Statement

Ride-sharing platforms generate thousands of ride transactions daily. Analyzing this large volume of data manually is difficult and time-consuming.

The business needs a centralized analytics solution to:

* Monitor revenue performance
* Track customer and booking trends
* Analyze vehicle utilization
* Identify high-performing locations
* Understand payment preferences
* Improve operational decision-making

This dashboard addresses these challenges through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## ❓ Key Business Questions

1. What is the total revenue generated from ride bookings?
2. Which vehicle category receives the highest number of bookings?
3. What is the average booking value?
4. Which payment method contributes the most revenue?
5. What percentage of bookings are successfully completed?
6. Which pickup locations generate the highest revenue?
7. How does booking value change across months?
8. Is there a relationship between ride distance and booking value?
9. Which regions have the highest ride demand?
10. What factors influence revenue growth?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Completed rides account for the majority of bookings.
* UPI is the most preferred payment method.
* Auto and Go Mini contribute significantly to booking volume.
* High-demand pickup locations generate a substantial portion of total revenue.
* Revenue varies across quarters, indicating seasonal trends.
* Longer ride distances generally result in higher booking values.

## 📌 Final Conclusion

The Uber Ride Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by integrating revenue, booking, customer, vehicle, and regional metrics into a single interactive reporting platform.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor business performance in real time
* Identify revenue-driving factors
* Understand customer booking behavior
* Optimize vehicle allocation
* Improve operational efficiency
* Make informed strategic decisions

This project demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw ride-booking data into actionable business insights.
